FAQ

Common questions

Do you work with early-stage startups?

Rvelate is built for organisations with 60–120 engineers that are scaling fast and under board or investor pressure. Earlier stage companies are typically better served by a part-time technical advisor.

What does "fixed-fee" mean in practice?

You agree a scope and a price upfront. No day rates, no time-billing, no surprise invoices. The fee is anchored to outcomes, not hours.

How quickly can an engagement start?

A Clarity Audit can typically begin within two weeks of agreement. Chidi takes on a limited number of engagements at any one time to ensure full focus.

What sectors do you work in?

Technology-led organisations across fintech, SaaS, payments, healthcare technology, and regulated scale-ups. Primarily US, UK, UAE, and Canada.

How is Rvelate™ different from a consulting firm?

Chidi works embedded inside your organisation — not as an external reporter. He sits in steering forums, architecture reviews, and leadership sessions. One person, full accountability.
